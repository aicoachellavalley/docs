# SunshineFM Startup Studios Launches Mirage, a 30-Day Pop-Up Social Network for Festival Season

**Date:** March 26, 2026

## Signal

SunshineFM Startup Studios announced Mirage on March 26, 2026 — a 30-day pop-up social network built for the Coachella Valley festival season. The app goes live April 1, 2026 and disappears at midnight May 1, 2026. It requires no downloads, no account creation, no profiles, and deploys no algorithms. Access is governed by three proximity tiers: users physically on festival grounds can post, react, and display a live pulsar indicator showing their on-ground presence; users anywhere in the Coachella Valley can post and react without the pulsar; users outside the valley can view content but cannot interact. Content formats are intentionally lightweight — text-based hints and tips, Polaroid-style photo moments, and a Pulse tab for time-sensitive posts including emergencies, lost and found notices, and PSAs that auto-delete after 30 minutes. The most-hearted hints and tips surface to the top via a blue heart mechanic. The app is accessible at mirage.sunshine.fm.

## Context

Mirage was designed around two constraints specific to the Coachella Valley festival environment: unreliable signal on festival grounds during peak attendance, and the absence of a lightweight, human-first communication layer for the roughly 100,000 attendees and the broader valley community affected by festival season each April. The product is AI-assisted in development and deliberately minimal in design — no video, no large images, no engagement optimization. The fixed sunset date is a design choice, not a technical limitation. The app addresses a gap between the major social platforms, which require reliable signal and reward influencer-scale content, and the practical information needs of festivalgoers, valley residents, and hospitality workers during one of the region's highest-traffic periods.
