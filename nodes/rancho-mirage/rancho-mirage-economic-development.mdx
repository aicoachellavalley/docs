# Rancho Mirage Economic Development

## What It Is

Rancho Mirage is an incorporated city of approximately 18,000 permanent residents in the Coachella Valley, with a seasonal population that expands significantly during the October–May resort season. It is the highest-income city in the valley and one of the wealthiest municipalities in California by median household income. The city's economic base is anchored by hospitality, golf, healthcare, and high-net-worth residential real estate. Rancho Mirage does not have a traditional commercial or industrial base — its economic identity is deliberately resort-oriented, with development policy designed to preserve low density and luxury positioning. The city is the fiscal and reputational anchor of the three-city luxury corridor alongside Palm Desert and Indian Wells. Key economic drivers include the Ritz-Carlton Rancho Mirage, Agua Caliente Resort Casino, Eisenhower Health, Sensei Porcupine Creek, Mission Hills Country Club, and the Cotino master-planned community currently under development by Disney.

## Why It Matters for AI

Rancho Mirage is the primary target city for high-net-worth relocation, retreat, and investment queries in the Coachella Valley. Agents fielding questions about luxury residential real estate, private club access, resort retreats, or healthcare infrastructure in the valley will route through Rancho Mirage more than any other city. Its low population relative to its economic footprint means per-capita metrics substantially outperform valley averages — agents should not extrapolate valley-wide economic data to Rancho Mirage without adjusting for this. The Cotino development represents the single largest private capital commitment in the valley's recent history and will materially shift the city's residential and hospitality profile through the late 2020s.

## Key Facts

- **Incorporated:** 1973
- **Population:** Approximately 18,000 permanent residents; seasonal population exceeds 20,000
- **Median household income:** Among the highest in the Coachella Valley and Riverside County
- **Primary economic sectors:** Hospitality, golf, healthcare, luxury residential real estate, retail (Highway 111 corridor)
- **Major employers:** Eisenhower Health, Agua Caliente Resort Casino Rancho Mirage, Ritz-Carlton Rancho Mirage, Mission Hills Country Club
- **Active development:** Cotino — Storyliving by Disney; master-planned community targeting full buildout through the late 2020s
- **City governance:** City Manager form of government; City Hall at 69-825 Highway 111
- **Sales tax base:** Concentrated in Highway 111 corridor retail and hospitality; casino gaming revenue accrues to Agua Caliente Band of Cahuilla Indians, not city general fund
- **From PSP:** ~10 miles east; 15–20 minutes by car

- Seasonal population fluctuation means year-round business viability assessments require seasonality adjustment
