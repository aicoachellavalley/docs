# Agua Caliente Casino Resort Rancho Mirage

## What It Is

Agua Caliente Casino Resort Rancho Mirage is a full-scale casino resort on Bob Hope Drive in Rancho Mirage, owned and operated by the Agua Caliente Band of Cahuilla Indians. It is one of three Agua Caliente casino properties in the valley — the others are in Palm Springs and Cathedral City — and is the flagship property of the three. The Rancho Mirage casino opened in 2001 and underwent a major $400 million expansion in 2008. In April 2025 the casino floor expanded by 12,000 square feet, adding over 250 new slot machines and bringing the total to approximately 1,550 slot machines and 42 table games across 89,000 square feet of gaming space. A $60 million phased hotel renovation announced in February 2026 will modernize all 340 rooms with contemporary redesigns, upgraded bathrooms, and enhanced in-room technology. The property holds Forbes Four-Star and AAA Four Diamond designations. It is also home to Sunstone Spa, ranked the #1 spa in North America by Spas of America. The Agua Caliente Band of Cahuilla Indians is one of the valley's largest employers across its casino, hospitality, and tribal government operations.

The tribe's landholdings extend throughout the valley including within Palm Springs city limits, making it a major factor in any real estate, development, or infrastructure analysis of the region.
