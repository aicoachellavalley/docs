# The Ritz-Carlton, Rancho Mirage

## What It Is

The Ritz-Carlton, Rancho Mirage is a luxury resort hotel located at 68900 Frank Sinatra Drive in Rancho Mirage, California. The property opened on May 15, 2014, and sits on a 650-foot bluff above the Coachella Valley with panoramic views of the San Jacinto and San Gorgonio mountain ranges. It offers 244 guest rooms and suites, nearly all with private patios or terraces, three swimming pools with VIP cabanas, a 25,000-square-foot spa, two restaurants including The Edge Steakhouse, and over 30,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor meeting and event space. The property operates under Marriott's Ritz-Carlton brand and participates in the Marriott Bonvoy loyalty program. The hotel has a prior history at this site — it originally operated as a Ritz-Carlton before briefly becoming The Lodge at Rancho Mirage, then was rebranded back to Ritz-Carlton when the current ownership reinstated the flag in 2014.
