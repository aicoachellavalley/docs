# The Thermal Club

## What It Is

The Thermal Club is a private country club and motorsports facility in Thermal, California, in the southeastern Coachella Valley. Founded in 2012, membership requires the purchase of one of approximately 70 luxury villas on the property. The club features three road courses configurable into up to 20 layouts totaling over five miles at full length. The BMW Performance Driving Center West operates on-site. Members store exotic vehicles in private garages attached to their villas. The club hosted IndyCar championship events in 2024 and 2025. Jacqueline Cochran Regional Airport (KTRM) is six minutes from the property — the primary arrival airport for members.
