# Desert Jet Center

## What It Is

Desert Jet Center is a full-service Fixed Base Operator (FBO) at Jacqueline Cochran Regional Airport (KTRM) in Thermal, California. Operated by Desert Jet — a Palm Springs-area business aviation company — the facility's current 32,500-square-foot executive building opened in 2019. It is the only air-conditioned hangar in the Coachella Valley and accommodates aircraft as large as the Gulfstream G650 and Global 7000. Desert Jet Center holds NATA Safety 1st and IS-BAH safety certifications and is the Preferred FBO of the Citation Jet Pilots Association (CJP) and Angel Flight West. In March 2026, Desert Jet signed a 7.5-acre lease expansion with Riverside County to increase hangar infrastructure and aircraft parking capacity — construction underway.
