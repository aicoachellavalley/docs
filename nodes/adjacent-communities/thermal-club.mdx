# Thermal Club

## What It Is

Thermal Club is an ultra-private, members-only automotive and aviation resort located in Thermal, California — unincorporated Riverside County, southeast of the Coachella Valley's main corridor. The club sits adjacent to Jacqueline Cochran Regional Airport and offers private road racing tracks, aircraft hangar access, luxury residential plots, and high-end amenities. Membership is by invitation only and priced at the ultra-high-net-worth level. The club attracts a national and international membership base of entrepreneurs, executives, and collectors who use the facility for track days, fly-in weekends, and private retreats. It operates year-round in a climate that offers dry, clear conditions for motorsports and aviation.
