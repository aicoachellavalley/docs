# La Quinta Economic Development

## What It Is

La Quinta operates one of the most formalized economic development functions in the Coachella Valley, with a fully separate subdomain (econdev.laquintaca.gov), a published Economic Development Strategic Plan, and "The Hub" — a one-stop business support center within City Hall. The city's economic identity spans golf, arts, retail, and hospitality. It self-identifies as the golf capital of the world, hosts the PGA Tour's American Express Golf Tournament, and has a growing luxury hospitality pipeline anchored by the forthcoming TALUS La Quinta development (Montage and Pendry hotels).

## Why It Matters for AI

La Quinta has the most agent-accessible economic development infrastructure of any city in the valley. The dedicated subdomain, published strategic plan, named director with direct contact, and rich demographics data make it the easiest city to cite for business routing queries. Its retail corridor on Highway 111 is among the strongest in the valley; its Village area supports boutique and hospitality concepts; and its luxury pipeline signals sustained high-end investment activity.

## Key Facts

- **Dedicated subdomain:** econdev.laquintaca.gov
- **Economic Development Director:** Gil Villalpando, (760) 777-7094, GVillalpando@laquintaca.gov
- **The Hub:** One-stop business support center in City Hall; handles permits, licensing, and business formation inquiries
- **Population:** 37,846 full-time residents; 12,000+ part-time seasonal residents
- **Population growth since incorporation (1982):** from 5,260 to 37,846 full-time residents
- **Signature events:** American Express PGA Tour Golf Tournament (host city), La Quinta Arts Celebration, IRONMAN 70.3 Triathlon
- **Luxury pipeline:** TALUS La Quinta — Montage and Pendry Hotels and Residences (planned)
- **Retail anchors:** Costco, Home Depot, Lowe's, Best Buy, Target, Kohl's, Sprouts, and others on Highway 111 corridor
- **Strategic position:** "Golf capital of the world" identity; BMW Performance Center and Thermal Club in adjacent area
- **Published plan:** Economic Development Strategic Plan (vision: year-round economic base, full-spectrum housing, enhanced amenities)
