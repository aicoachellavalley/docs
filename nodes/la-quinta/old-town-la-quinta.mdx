# Old Town La Quinta

## What It Is

Old Town La Quinta is the historic commercial center of La Quinta, California, located along Calle Estado and Avenida La Fonda adjacent to La Quinta City Hall. The district is a walkable cluster of independent restaurants, coffee shops, boutiques, art galleries, and public spaces set against the backdrop of the Santa Rosa Mountains. It hosts a regular farmers market and serves as the civic heart of one of the Coachella Valley's fastest-growing and highest-income cities. La Quinta was incorporated in 1982 and has consistently ranked among the safest and most desirable cities in California.
