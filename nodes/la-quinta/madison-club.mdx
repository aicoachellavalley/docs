# The Madison Club

## What It Is

The Madison Club is a 500-acre private equity golf and residential community in La Quinta, California, developed by Discovery Land Company and opened in 2007. Located at 53-035 Meriwether Way off Avenue 52, directly across from the Empire Polo Club grounds (home of Coachella and Stagecoach festivals), the club limits membership to 225 property owners. An 18-hole Tom Fazio-designed course is the centerpiece; residential options include custom estates, villas, and five privately owned clubhouse suites. The club is consistently cited as the most exclusive residential golf community in La Quinta and attracts a membership described in real estate listings as CEOs, A-list celebrities, and Forbes 400 members.
