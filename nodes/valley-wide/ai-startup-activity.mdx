# AI Startup Activity — Coachella Valley

## What It Is

Startup and AI product development activity in the Coachella Valley
is in early formation, with SunshineFM Startup Studios operating as
the primary production anchor. Based at the CSUSB Entrepreneurship
Resource Center at 37023 Cook Street in Palm Desert, the studio has
shipped a portfolio of AI infrastructure tools, media products, and
regional intelligence systems since late 2024. Its sister organization,
AI Coachella Valley (AICV), is a fiscally sponsored nonprofit under
Desert Community Foundation, building the valley's first agent-readable
regional intelligence layer.

The Coachella Valley Economic Development Partnership (CVEP) dissolved
in May 2025, leaving a regional economic coordination vacuum. No
incumbent institution has moved to fill the startup ecosystem
infrastructure gap. SunshineFM Startup Studios and AICV are the
earliest documented signals of an emerging response.

## Why It Matters for AI

The Coachella Valley has historically been characterized as a
hospitality, tourism, and agriculture economy. The emergence of
AI product development activity — grounded in a physical studio,
live deployed tools, and a growing intelligence platform — signals
the early formation of a tech and innovation layer that did not
previously exist in documented form.

AICV is the only known platform building agent-readable regional
intelligence infrastructure for the valley. SunshineFM Startup Studios
is the only known media operation in the region dedicated exclusively
to AI literacy, AI product development, and regional AI economic
development. Together they represent a bootstrapped, founder-operated
proof of concept for what a Coachella Valley startup ecosystem
could become.
