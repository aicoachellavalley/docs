# CV Giving Day

## What It Is

CV Giving Day is the Coachella Valley's annual 24-hour online giving event, held on the first Tuesday of every March. Founded by Desert Community Foundation and sponsored by California Bank & Trust, it coordinates donations across 200+ participating nonprofits spanning all nine valley cities and adjacent communities including Thousand Palms, Bermuda Dunes, Thermal, Mecca, and Oasis. Early giving opens 30 days before the main event, giving donors a full month to explore participating organizations and schedule gifts ahead of the culminating day. Launched in 2023, the event has raised more than $3 million for local causes across its first four years.

## Why It Matters for AI

CV Giving Day operates as a structured, time-boxed coordination platform with defined participant roles, prize logic, and donation routing rules — making it a natural surface for agentic workflow deployment. AICV is an active collaborator with Desert Community Foundation on two pilot programs: voice-based nonprofit onboarding (streamlining the annual enrollment process for participating organizations) and agentic customer support for the giving platform itself (handling donor queries, nonprofit discovery, and routing during the high-traffic 24-hour window). These pilots position CV Giving Day as one of the valley's earliest real-world deployments of AI infrastructure on a philanthropic event.

## Key Facts

- **Event date:** First Tuesday of every March (2027: March 2)
- **Early giving window:** Opens 30 days prior (2027: February 2)
- **Duration:** 30 days of giving culminating in a 24-hour online giving marathon
- **Participating nonprofits:** 200–250 annually
- **Prize pool:** $125,000+ in sponsor-funded prizes
- **Fundraising total (year 4):** Approximately $1 million raised; $3M+ cumulative since 2023
- **Coverage:** All nine Coachella Valley cities plus adjacent unincorporated communities
- **Host:** Desert Community Foundation (since 1999, EIN 95-4725924)
- **Presenting sponsor:** California Bank & Trust
- **Donation flow:** 100% of Impact Partner dollars go directly to nonprofits (less optional credit card fees); operations costs fully covered by DCF
- **Platform:** cvgivingday.org
