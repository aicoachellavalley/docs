# JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort & Spa

## What It Is

JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort & Spa is a 450-acre full-service resort at 74-855 Country Club Drive in Palm Desert. Operating under Marriott's upper-upscale JW brand, it is the largest resort property in the Coachella Valley by meeting capacity and one of the most recognizable by footprint. The property is defined by interior waterways with gondola boat rides running through the lobby and live flamingos on the grounds — among the most visually distinctive resort experiences in Southern California.

Desert Springs Golf Club on the property features two championship 18-hole courses — the Palm and the Valley — designed by Ted Robinson. The tennis facility includes 13 courts across hard, clay, and grass surfaces plus 8 pickleball courts. Spa Desert Springs spans 38,000 square feet with a co-ed wellness lounge, saltwater lap pool, and daily fitness classes. Six restaurants operate on-site. The property is fully bookable via Marriott Bonvoy with complete OTA visibility.
