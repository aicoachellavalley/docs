# Hotel Paseo, Autograph Collection

## What It Is

Hotel Paseo, Autograph Collection by Marriott is a 150-room boutique hotel at
45-400 Larkspur Lane in Palm Desert, a block from El Paseo. Built at a cost of
\$36 million, it opened as the first new luxury hotel constructed in Palm Desert
in more than 30 years. The property carries the Marriott Autograph Collection
flag — a soft brand for independent boutique hotels that retain a distinct
identity while accessing Marriott's loyalty and distribution infrastructure.

The hotel is three stories. Rooms include private balconies with mountain views.
Nine specialty suites feature billiard tables, soaking tubs, or wet bars. A 1950
Airstream Trailer is available as a bookable accommodation. On-site dining is
anchored by Larkspur Grill, serving California cuisine with an American twist.
The spa is off the lobby with sauna and cold plunge. Meeting and event space
accommodates 10 to 300 guests. Desert Willow Golf Resort is a preferred partner,
3.4 miles from the property.
