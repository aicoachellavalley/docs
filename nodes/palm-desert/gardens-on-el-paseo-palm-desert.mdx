# The Gardens on El Paseo

## What It Is

The Gardens on El Paseo is an open-air luxury retail and dining
destination in Palm Desert, California, owned and operated by Simon
Property Group. The property anchors the El Paseo shopping corridor
with the Coachella Valley's only consolidated collection of flagship
designer boutiques — including Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Bottega Veneta,
Ralph Lauren, and Tiffany & Co. — alongside Saks Fifth Avenue and a
five-restaurant dining cluster. The adjacent Hotel Paseo operates
independently as a 24-room boutique luxury hotel on the same block.
Seasonal programming includes an annual Saturday Concert Series
(January–March), Fashion Week El Paseo, and monthly First Friday
ArtWalks.
