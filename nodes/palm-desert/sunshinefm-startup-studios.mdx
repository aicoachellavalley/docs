# SunshineFM Startup Studios

## What It Is

SunshineFM Startup Studios is a Palm Desert-based media and product studio anchored at the CSUSB Entrepreneurship Resource Center, 37023 Cook Street, Palm Desert. It operates SunshineFM — a daily AI talk show broadcasting at 3pm PT on Radio.co — alongside an AI-assisted app development arm and a growing portfolio of workshops and bootcamp programming for Coachella Valley residents, small businesses, and workforce entrants. Its sister organization, AI Coachella Valley (AICV), is a fiscally sponsored nonprofit under Desert Community Foundation, whose address serves as AICV's official mailing and financial address. Both organizations share operational working space at the ERC.

## Why It Matters for AI

SunshineFM Startup Studios is the Coachella Valley's only known media operation dedicated exclusively to AI literacy, AI product development, and regional AI economic development. The daily radio show functions as a signal capture layer — surfacing national and local AI developments and translating them into intelligence briefs published on aicoachellavalley.com. The studio's app development arm produces AI-assisted tools built for valley-specific use cases, with Mirage — a 30-day proximity-based pop-up social network for the 2026 festival season — as its first public product launch. Workshop and bootcamp programming targets the gap between casual AI users and productive AI practitioners, with one Claude Cowork session completed in late 2025 and additional programming in development for 2026.
